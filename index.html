How it works

Simple flow, optimized for speed

User places an order
Browse essentials, add to cart, choose address, pay on delivery/online (future).
Shop owner confirms & packs
Accept the order, pack items, update availability if needed.
Delivery partner picks up & delivers
Pickup verification, route view, status updates, proof of delivery.
Admin monitors & improves
Manage users/shops/partners, resolve issues, view analytics.
